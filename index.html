Capoeira for Beginners

In these early morning sessions we train fundamental kicks and escapes, magical ginga steps, and a few other basic movements essential to expressing yourself in a Capoeira game. These movements look easy, but they are truly difficult to master. Mastery can take years, but no matter how long the journey takes, training Capoeira is always a fun and challenging workout filled with activities that make great and lasting friendships.

CDO Jamaica

Instrutor Zumbi, born in Jamaica and founder of Quilombola Recreation in Korea, wants to start a branch of the Cordão de Ouro Capoeira group in Trelawny, on the north coast of Jamaica. Zumbi leads the Cordão de Ouro Seoul Capoeira group but he needs your donations so that he can get the project started. Zumbi is looking to raise about $5,000 USD to purchase tickets to the island for Mestre Chicote and Contra Mestre Xango. Once on the island, these specially selected teachers will teach Capoeira to kids on the beach.
